# Formi - AI Form Builder
#### Build Any Form, Power Any Idea—AI at Your Fingertips.


## Overview
Unleash the power of AI with **Formi**, the ultimate form builder script that lets you create **any type of form or AI generator** effortlessly. Whether you're building custom AI tools or innovative solutions, Formi empowers you to bring your ideas to life in minutes—no coding required.

## Features
- **AI-Powered Form Creation:** Automatically generate forms and AI tools with smart suggestions and pre-built templates.
- **Customizable Templates:** Choose from a variety of templates or create your own from scratch.
- **Build Any AI Generator:** Create unique AI tools tailored to your specific needs—the possibilities are endless.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive drag-and-drop functionality for seamless form building.
- **Responsive Design:** Forms and generators look great on all devices—desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **Advanced Customization:** Add logic-based branching, conditional questions, and custom themes.
- **Data Security:** Built with encryption and privacy protocols to keep your data safe.

## Requirements
To run **Formi - AI-Powered Form Builder Script**, ensure your server meets the following requirements:

- **PHP Version:** 7.4 or higher
- **SQLite:** Enabled (for database functionality)
- **Web Server**: Apache or Nginx (recommended)
- **PHP Extensions:** PDO, JSON, cURL, and OpenSSL (enabled by default in most PHP installations)
- **Browser Support:** Modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
